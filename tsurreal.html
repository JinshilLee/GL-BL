Meet Tsurreal, the 22-year-old music producer born and raised in Hawaii. Tsurreal's journey into music began early, inspired by the rich cultural tapestry of the islands.

Having recently collaborated with A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie on his acclaimed album "Better Off Alone," Tsurreal has quickly established a reputation for crafting dynamic and emotive soundscapes. His work on the album showcased his versatility and keen sense of musicality, setting a high standard in the competitive realm of hip-hop and R&B production.

Currently, Tsurreal is honing his craft and focusing on collaborating with top-tier artists such as Bryson Tiller, SZA, H.E.R, and Chris Brown.
