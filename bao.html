Based in Montreal, Canada, Michael Bao is a 23-year-old artist whose music is a captivating fusion of Alternative and Alternative Pop, blending soulful acoustic vibes with Kacey Musgraves and Soccer Mommy inspirations. His melodies resonate with a distinct emotive depth that draws listeners into his world.

Apart from his own musical pursuits, Michael is deeply committed to artist development, specializing in songwriting and production. His passion for nurturing talent is evident in his collaboration with CHRISFIFTYSIX, where he actively supports and mentors emerging artists, helping them refine their craft and find their unique voice.

In Montreal's dynamic music scene, Michael Bao's innovative approach and dedication to pushing creative boundaries set him apart. With each composition, he continues to evolve and explore new sonic territories, leaving a lasting impression with his heartfelt lyrics and compelling melodies.
