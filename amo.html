Introducing Amo, the 20-year-old music producer making waves from the vibrant city of Toronto. Amo has swiftly carved out a niche for himself in the competitive world of hip-hop production highlighted by his collaboration with EST Gee on "I Can't Feel a Thing," where his production skills contributed to the track's compelling narrative and mood.

Specializing in crafting beats and loops with a distinctively dark, hard, and unique ambient style, Amo's sound is characterized by impactful, bouncy 808s layered with ambient or dark undertones. His compositions blend elements of urban grit with a keen sense of atmospheric depth.

Beyond his notable collaborations, Amo has also cultivated unreleased songs with rising artists such as Hunxho and Nardo Wick, further solidifying his reputation as a sought-after producer within the industry.
